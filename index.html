Tribute to Narendra Modi

Prime Minister of India and a visionary leader

About Narendra Modi

Narendra Damodardas Modi is the current and 14th Prime Minister of India, having assumed office on May 26, 2014. Born on September 17, 1950, in Vadnagar, Gujarat, Modi is known for his decisive leadership, economic reforms, and efforts to enhance India's global stature. He is also recognized for initiatives like Digital India, Make in India, and Swachh Bharat Abhiyan.

Timeline of Achievements

  • 1950 - Born in Vadnagar, Gujarat, India
  • 2001 - Became Chief Minister of Gujarat
  • 2014 - Sworn in as the 14th Prime Minister of India
  • 2016 - Launched the Goods and Services Tax (GST) and Demonetization
  • 2019 - Re-elected as Prime Minister with a massive mandate

Legacy

Narendra Modi’s leadership has transformed India through various socio-economic reforms, technological advancements, and enhanced international relations. His campaigns for a cleaner, more digital, and more self-reliant India have left a significant impact on the nation’s growth and global image.

This tribute page is dedicated to Mr. Narendra Modi, a leader committed to the progress and prosperity of India.
